REMOVE ACCOUNT - NOTICE

This feature completely removes the account from this device.

The account will not be eliminated or destroyed, but instead will not be shown anymore in the Accounts List at the client startup.

You can always re-generate your account on this device again using the Import Account feature and entering the SEED when prompted.

Click on the REMOVE button to proceed with the account removal, or CANCEL to return to the previous screen.


NEW ACCOUNT - NOTICE

This feature involves the creation of a new account on Waves platform.

The procedure consists of complex cryptographic operations that use a SEED (a series of random words) to create a unique address for your account.

This SEED is extremely important and must be kept in safe place, since it's the only way to recover your account in case of lost, corrupted data or hardware failure.

Your SEED will also allow you to IMPORT your account to other devices.

NOTE: Make sure that you select the entire SEED lenght before keeping it. It's suggested to use the copy-to-clipboard button located the right side of the SEED box.

PORTFOLIO


The Portfolio area serves both as an analytic tool and a summary overview of a user's holdings. It is the perfect place to draft market strategies and plan future investments.

The Portfolio tab will start operating as Waves platform incorporates additional markets, meanwhile the transactions can be tracked via History tab.



DECENTRALIZED EXCHANGE


Waves' features include a decentralized exchange in which users will be able to trade tokens of all kinds. There will be no AML/KYC requirements in order to use this service.

The Decentralized Exchange is scheduled for launch in the last quarter of 2016.



DECENTRALIZED VOTING


With a fair and automated system, Waves' voting area enables communities and projects to achieve consensus for critical decision-making that might otherwise prove difficult or controversial. A full set of analytic tools will also be available to follow up the voting process in real time.

The Decentralized Voting feature is scheduled for launch in the final quarter of 2016.
